# 015_multiple_suppliers - Multiple Suppliers

## Business Scenario

Multiple supplier purchases and payment

## Accounting Purpose

This fixture validates deterministic double-entry posting for `Multiple Suppliers`.

## Expected Accounting Outcome

- Total debits: 3100.0
- Total credits: 3100.0
- Net profit: -2600.0
- Voucher count: 4

## Reports Expected To Change

- Trial Balance
- Ledger Balances
- Profit & Loss when income or expense ledgers are present
- Balance Sheet when asset, liability, or equity ledgers are present
