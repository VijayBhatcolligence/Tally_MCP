# 016_inventory_purchase_sale - Inventory Purchase Sale

## Business Scenario

Inventory purchase, sale and COGS

## Accounting Purpose

This fixture validates deterministic double-entry posting for `Inventory Purchase Sale`.

## Expected Accounting Outcome

- Total debits: 2300.0
- Total credits: 2300.0
- Net profit: 500.0
- Voucher count: 3

## Reports Expected To Change

- Trial Balance
- Ledger Balances
- Profit & Loss when income or expense ledgers are present
- Balance Sheet when asset, liability, or equity ledgers are present
