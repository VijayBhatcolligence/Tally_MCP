# 014_multiple_customers - Multiple Customers

## Business Scenario

Multiple customer credit sales and receipt

## Accounting Purpose

This fixture validates deterministic double-entry posting for `Multiple Customers`.

## Expected Accounting Outcome

- Total debits: 3700.0
- Total credits: 3700.0
- Net profit: 3000.0
- Voucher count: 4

## Reports Expected To Change

- Trial Balance
- Ledger Balances
- Profit & Loss when income or expense ledgers are present
- Balance Sheet when asset, liability, or equity ledgers are present
